# Banana Glove & Monkey Swing 

## An interactive wearable game experience for piano teachers and their very young students to collobaratively engage in therapy and flexbility exercises. The game incorporates storytelling and humor to engage the young and a delightful human centered experience to make physical therapy more compelling for the adult. 

![cover image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/35578913/39736778-8afd698c-5236-11e8-9cc3-4dde6fcf856a.jpg)

## Project Goal

What would a wearable therapy device look like if it were context specific? A key component of this project was developing an educational therapy wearable that strengthened the relationship between student and teacher. The teacher achieves their therapy exercises while the student practices their wrist flexbility piano warm ups.
